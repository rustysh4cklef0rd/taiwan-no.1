Stacked 學字 (learn characters) — red on top, cyan below. Two-color neon vertical sign. Evokes a Taiwan night market sign.

Design Rationale

Cleanest Small Size

台 is visually simpler than 學 — fewer strokes = better legibility at 20–40px notification icon size. The "TWN" sub-label disappears gracefully at small sizes.

BEST SMALL-SIZE

Character Choice

台 = Taiwan (origin of Traditional script). Anchors the app's identity to Traditional Chinese as used in Taiwan, clearly distinguishing it from Simplified Chinese tools.

Neon Sign Anatomy

Real neon signs: bright white-hot core, colored mid-glow, wide diffuse falloff. CSS approximation: text-shadow layers at 2px white / 8px color / 20px color 80% opacity / 40px color 40% opacity.

Implementation Notes

For Android: export as adaptive icon. Foreground: character+glow on transparent. Background: #0D0D0F. For iOS: flat PNG at 1024×1024, no alpha. Glow effect baked in.
